HI sky maps

Coarse maps of extended emission built by raster- or drift-scanning the sky. The dish is a single ~21° beam, so these are beam-limited maps of large-scale structure (galactic hydrogen, the Sun) — a low-resolution intensity/velocity chart, not a resolved image. An az/el map is filled by the rotator raster; a galactic map is filled by ingesting a drift-scan campaign's passes.

New map

Centre/extent/step are in the grid frame's degrees (galactic l/b, or az/el). Step ≈ half the beam (~10°) samples it ~Nyquist. Dwell is the per-cell integration for the raster.
